## Key Implementation Details

### 1. Impact Calculation

Monthly frequency is normalized with simple MVP multipliers: daily 22, weekly 4, monthly 1, occasional 0.5. Monthly hours and costs are rounded to two decimals.

### 2. Classification

Classification uses keyword groups over normalized title, description, and area text. The fallback is `unclassified`.

### 3. Automation Potential

Manual repetitive work, duplicated data, manual approvals, and missing integrations map to high automation potential.

### 4. Priority

Priority uses a small score from monthly hours, estimated cost, pain level, and automation potential. High pain plus high impact also forces high priority.

## Decisions Made

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Rule implementation | Pure TypeScript services | Easy to test and reuse from APIs. |
| Frequency multipliers | daily 22, weekly 4, monthly 1, occasional 0.5 | Practical MVP assumptions. |
| Fallback category | `unclassified` | Avoids pretending a weak keyword match is reliable. |
| Priority threshold | Score >= 6 or high pain with high impact | Keeps prioritization simple and explainable. |
